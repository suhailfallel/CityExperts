FAQ

1. Q: Is your pest control service safe for my family and pets?

A: Yes, we prioritize the safety of your family and pets. Our pest control solutions use eco-friendly and low-toxicity products that are safe for humans and pets when applied according to our expert technicians' guidelines.

2. Q: How often should I schedule pest control treatments for my home?

A: The frequency of pest control treatments depends on various factors, such as the level of infestation, property type, and environmental conditions. We recommend periodic inspections and tailored treatment plans based on your specific needs.

3. Q: What pests do your services cover?

A: Our comprehensive pest control services cover a wide range of pests, including cockroaches, ants, bed bugs, termites, rodents, mosquitoes, and more. We are equipped to handle both common household pests and specialized pest challenges.

4. Q: Are your technicians licensed and trained?

A: Yes, our pest control technicians are highly trained, certified, and experienced professionals. They undergo regular training to stay updated with the latest pest control techniques and safety protocols.

5. Q: Can I get a quote for pest control services without a home inspection?

A: While an on-site inspection is often necessary for an accurate quote, we can provide a preliminary estimate based on the information you provide during our initial consultation. However, we highly recommend an inspection for personalized recommendations.

6. Q: How long does a typical pest control treatment take?

A: The duration of the treatment depends on the pest issue's severity and the size of the property. On average, most treatments take between 1 to 2 hours. Our technicians will inform you of the estimated time during the appointment.

7. Q: Do you offer emergency pest control services?

A: Yes, we understand that some pest issues require immediate attention. We provide emergency pest control services to address urgent situations promptly.

8. Q: Will I need to vacate my property during pest control treatment?

A: In most cases, you and your family can stay in your home during the treatment. However, for specific pest control procedures, temporary evacuation may be recommended. Our technicians will provide guidance based on the treatment plan.

9. Q: How do I prepare my home for pest control treatment?

A: Our team will provide you with detailed preparation instructions before the scheduled treatment. Common steps include clearing clutter, covering food items, and relocating pets to a safe area.

10. Q: Do you offer any guarantees on your pest control services?

A: Yes, we stand behind the quality of our services. We offer satisfaction guarantees to ensure our clients are fully satisfied with the results of our pest control treatments.
